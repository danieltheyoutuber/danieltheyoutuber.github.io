Friends Kyo Hostel

Featuring free bikes, shared lounge, garden and free WiFi, Friends Kyo Hostel is located in Kyoto, 1.2 km from Samurai Kembu Kyoto and 1.6 km from Sanjusangen-do Temple. The property is situated 1.9 km from Kiyomizu-dera Temple, 2 km from Shoren-in Temple and 2.4 km from Kyoto International Manga Museum. The property offers city views, a terrace and a 24-hour front desk. The units in the hostel are equipped with a kettle. Rooms at Friends Kyo Hostel have river views and a shared bathroom with a bidet and a hairdryer. All rooms have air conditioning and a private bathroom. At Friends Kyo Hostel you can play darts. Heian Shrine is 2.5 km from the hostel, while TKP Garden City Kyoto is 2.6 km away. The nearest airport is Itami Airport, 39 km from Friends Kyo Hostel.
