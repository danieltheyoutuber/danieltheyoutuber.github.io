Welcome Japan Explorers!







Hello there fellow Japanese Explores! Welcome to the official japanese tourist website. From here you can see the different places where you can visit, eat or stay in your next trip to Japan. In our website you'll have the oportunity to see in advance historic places like: Mount Fuji, Imperial Tokyo, Hiroshima Peace Memorial Park, Osaka Castle, Fushimi Inari-taisha Shrine in Kyoto and many more. If you're intrested more in tasting the Japanese cuisine you can see beforehand some of the specialities of their cuisine and where you can buy them like Buta-No-Shogayaki (Ginger Pork), Okonomiyaki (considered the Japanese frittata or pancake), Onigiri (Japanese rice ball), among others delicious plates. Below, you'll see a photo carousel with some of the places and foods mentioned above; we hope you spend a great time in your next trip to Japan!
